Modeling Work

In 2020 her aunt de ella asks her to be a model for her clothing brand.She accepted, did the shooting and instantly loved it. She asked her mom to sign with some agencies to continue doing it. In February 2021 she did a shooting with Mariel Salfi, a photographer in Rosario, and with that photos she got featured in Forager, as one of the best female portraits. Following that, she got featured in her first print international Magazine: Bahia Kids Magazine! She shooted for some locals brands at that time. After that, she booked a modeling job for Buddies (a big brand for kids and teens in Argentina) followed by an Editorial for Como Quieres Ar, one of the leading brand for teens in Argentina. Later, she signed a contract with 47Street, one of the most famous brands in Argentina for youth! This allowed her to be featured on various pages of international models, and to appear in international stores such as "Dafiti" among others in South America.
