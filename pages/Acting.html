Acting Skills

She started with Acting at the age of 4, studying Musical Comedy (theater) classes in “Estudio de comedias musicales” en Fisherton, Rosario. She took classes for 8 years in that academy. Each of those years preformed in 1 or 2 plays per year. She got graduated of Musical Comedy at the age of 9, with her first lead role, playing the role of ‘Belen’ in ‘A Christmas Story’ the musical. With that, at the age of 13, when COVID-19 started, she had get more interested in acting for screen and modeling. She started studying acting for screen, had done her first headshots, and started doing auditions. She have done a lot of workshops with casting directors, actors, and acting coaches. She took (and still taking) acting classes in various academics: “Estudio Moloco” , “ECM house”, “Estudio de comedias musicales”, “Artemisa Kids”, “LeBlanc School of acting”, and others. In 2021 she performed in some MusicVideos.
