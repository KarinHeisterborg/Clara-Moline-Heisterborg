Dancing

She started with dance at the age of 4, in Fisherton Musical Comedy Studio. She started dancing various styles, including Jazz, Ballet, Musical Theater, Contemporary, Pop, and others. She continued in that studio for 7 years. At the age of 11, she started Gymnastics, and at 12 she became a part of the competition team in “Centro asturiano de Rosario”. That helped her with the Acro, and they also took ballet classes once a week there. She was a competitive gymnast for 3 years. In 2021, she left gymnastics and started Dance at ECM House Fisherton. In there, she learned different styles (Kpop, Pop, Lyrical, Hip Hop). In 2021 she also started Dance at “Musas Institute” the academy in which he continues today, in which he learned (and continue learning) Ballet, Contemporary Jazz, and Acro.
